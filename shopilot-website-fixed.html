🛒 Our Story

Built for Indian restaurants,
by people who care

ShoPilot was born from a simple idea — that every restaurant owner in India deserves smart, affordable technology to run their business better.

💡 Why We Built ShoPilot

We saw restaurant owners juggling paper order slips, missed orders, and manual billing every single day. Existing software was either too expensive, too complex, or built for the West. We wanted to change that. ShoPilot was designed from the ground up for Indian restaurants — with UPI payments, GST invoicing, and a waiter panel that works on any smartphone, no installation needed.

🎯 Our Mission

To make every restaurant in India — from a small dhaba in Agra to a multi-table café in Delhi — run as efficiently as the best in the world. We believe technology should be simple, fast, and actually useful. That's what we build.

🚀 Our Vision

We're just getting started. Our vision is to become the operating system for restaurants across India — powering orders, payments, inventory, and analytics for thousands of restaurants in every city and town. We want to give every restaurant owner the same tools that big chains have, at a price that makes sense.
